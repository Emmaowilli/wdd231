Site Name

Name: EcoUganda Journeys

Reason: This name combines 'Eco' for sustainability and 'Uganda Journeys' to reflect travel experiences, emphasizing Uganda‚Äôs unique eco-tourism offerings and appealing to environmentally conscious travelers.

Optional Domain: ecougandajourneys.org

Site Purpose

The EcoUganda Journeys website will promote sustainable travel in Uganda by providing detailed information on national parks (e.g., Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, Queen Elizabeth National Park), natural attractions (e.g., Lake Bunyonyi), and eco-friendly activities. It will include a dynamic weather widget, a destinations page with descriptions and galleries, and a plan your trip page with travel tips, a contact form, and an interactive map of park locations.

Scenarios

  • What are the best eco-friendly lodges near Bwindi Impenetrable Forest for gorilla trekking?
  • How can I plan a sustainable trip to Lake Bunyonyi with local guides?

Color Schema

The site will use a natural, earthy palette to reflect Uganda‚Äôs landscapes:

  • #2E7D32 (Forest Green): Used for headings and buttons to symbolize nature and sustainability.
  • #F9E79F (Sand Yellow): Used for backgrounds and accents to evoke the savannah and warmth.

Typography

The site will use the following fonts for a natural and readable design:

  • Lora: Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) to create a rustic, organic feel suitable for eco-tourism.
  • Roboto: Used for body text (paragraphs, lists) for clarity and modern accessibility.
